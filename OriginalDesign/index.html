Abstract

A pattern of abstract growing squares and a smiley face cursor.

a: Toggle random colors.
w: Speed up the squares (maximum of 10).
s: Slow down the squares (minimum of 0).
e: Increase the smiley face size (maximum of 2040).
d: Decrease the smiley face size (minimum of 60).
r: Reset to the default state.
