Starfield

.

Experiment with different explosions in the starfield by using the mouse and keyboard.

Mouse: Create an explosion.
a: Hold while clicking to make the explosion spin clockwise.
s: Hold while clicking to make the explosion spin counter-clockwise.
g: Hold while clicking to make the explosion constantly create new dots.
h: Toggle if the next explosion will 'hover' (description of what that is below).
1: Clear the screen and set up a preset example.
r: Clear the screen.

Hover means that the dots will speed up when the mouse hovers over them. This can be used to make an outline of a circle or ellipse.

Note: There is a limit of 10 explosions on the screen at one time to prevent excessive lag.
