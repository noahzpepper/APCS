Sierpinski Triangle

An interactive Sierpinski Triangle.

If you're looking for a challenge, try and make your triangle as equilateral as possible. Then, press spacebar to see how close you are.

WASD: Control the triangle.
Q: Toggle control style between movement and growth.
H: Show and hide the controllable Sierpinski Triangle.
Spacebar: Show and hide an equilateral Sierpinski Triangle.
Click: Toggle the color scheme.
